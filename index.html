My Childhood

Hobbies

Dancing

I enjoyed dancing so much as a kid that I led every single dance team I was a part of.

I took ballroom and ballet classes in Primary School.

Singing

I enjoyed singing too. Although I can't sing, I sang all the time and even wanted to go for singing lessons.

I cannot sing to save my life but I was also in the school choir in Grade 5.

Toys

Baby Born Doll

As a kid, I always saw ads about Baby Born Dolls and wanted one so bad. My Grandpa bought me my first one.

It was a surprise purchase. I will never forget that day, I was so happy!

Teddy Bear

My first and favourite teddy bear was named Rosie.

I took Rosie everywhere with me, even to school!
